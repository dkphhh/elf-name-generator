## üßù D&D Elf Subraces Overview

### High Elf

**Racial Traits:**

- +2 Dexterity, +1 Intelligence
- Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry
- Trance instead of sleep
- Proficiency in longswords and bows
- **Bonus Wizard Cantrip** - extra magic at level 1

**Best Classes:**

- Wizard (Intelligence synergy)
- Eldritch Knight Fighter
- Arcane Trickster Rogue
- Ranger with magical flair

**Name Examples from Our Generator:**

- Male: Aelion Starweaver, Theren Moonblade, Elarion Swiftwind
- Female: Valanthe Silverlight, Mialee Starwhisper, Elysia Dawnbrook

### Wood Elf

**Racial Traits:**

- +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
- Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry
- **Fleet of Foot** - 35 ft. movement speed
- Mask of the Wild - hide in natural phenomena

**Best Classes:**

- Ranger (perfect synergy)
- Druid (Wisdom-based)
- Monk (mobile and agile)
- Rogue (stealth master)

**Name Examples:**

- Male: Adran Leafwhisper, Enialis Wildrunner, Carric Thornbow
- Female: Naivara Greenmantle, Meriele Swiftarrow, Enna Forestsong

### Dark Elf (Drow)

**Racial Traits:**

- +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
- Superior Darkvision (120 ft.)
- Sunlight Sensitivity (disadvantage in sunlight)
- **Drow Magic** - Dancing Lights, Faerie Fire, Darkness

**Best Classes:**

- Warlock (Charisma-based, thematic)
- Rogue (perfect stats)
- Paladin (redemption arc)
- Bard (Charisma synergy)

**Name Examples:**

- Male: Drizzt'ar Nightblade, Zelenor Darkweaver, Vorn'ath Shadowstep

**Racial Traits:**

- +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
- Fey Step - teleportation ability
- **Seasonal Forms** - change with long rests
- Enhanced magical nature

**Best Classes:**

- Bard (teleport + spellcasting)
- Sorcerer (Fey Touched origin)
- Warlock (Archfey patron)

## üìú Creating Compelling Elf Backstories

### High Elf Background Ideas

1. **Academic Prodigy** - Left the prestigious university to seek practical knowledge
2. **Exiled Noble** - Disgraced family member seeking redemption
3. **War Veteran** - Survivor of an ancient conflict haunted by memories
4. **Magical Researcher** - Searching for lost elven artifacts

### Wood Elf Background Ideas

1. **Tribal Scout** - First contact with civilization
2. **Beast Speaker** - Special connection with forest animals
3. **Exile** - Broke a sacred forest law, seeking atonement
4. **Guardian** - Protecting the forest from encroaching civilization

### Drow Background Ideas

1. **Surface Refugee** - Fled the cruelty of Underdark society
2. **Heretic** - Rejected Lolth, seeking new purpose
3. **Spy** - Infiltrated surface world, but grew to love it
4. **Lost Child** - Raised by surface dwellers, never knew the Underdark

## üé≤ Elf Character Optimization Tips

### Ability Score Priorities

**For Martial Elves (Fighter, Ranger, Rogue):**

1. Dexterity (primary combat stat)
2. Constitution (survivability)
3. Wisdom (for Rangers, perception)

**For Magical Elves (Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock):**

1. Intelligence/Charisma (spellcasting)
2. Dexterity (AC and initiative)
3. Constitution (concentration saves)

### Feat Recommendations

**Elven Accuracy** (Xanathar's Guide)

- Perfect for Dexterity builds
- Triple advantage on attack rolls
- Best for Rogues and Rangers

**Fey Touched**

- Extra spells and ability boost
- Thematic for all elves
- Misty Step is incredibly useful

**Sharpshooter**

- Ideal for Wood Elf Rangers
- Compensate with advantage from hiding
- Massive damage potential

**War Caster**

- Essential for Eldritch Knights
- Maintain concentration in combat
- Cast with weapons drawn

### Authentic D&D Elf Name Patterns

**High Elf Names:**

- Elegant, 2-3 syllables
- Common endings: -ion, -iel, -is, -as, -or
- Examples: Soveliss, Immeral, Aelar, Mindartis

**Wood Elf Names:**

- Natural, flowing sounds
- Often shorter than High Elf names
- Examples: Adran, Eralian, Carric, Heian

**Drow Names:**

- Harsher consonants
- Apostrophes in surnames (house names)
- Examples: Drizzt, Rizzen, Zakafein, Malice
